Espresso

A strong, black coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans, resulting in a rich and intense flavor with a layer of cream on top.

Latte

A smooth and creamy coffee made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a small amount of foam. It's a perfect for those who enjoy a softer coffee flavor.

Capuccinno

A classic Italian coffee made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It has a rich, bold flavor with a creamy texture and a frothy, velvety top.

Cold brew

A smooth and refreshing coffee brewed slowly with cold water over an extended period. mellow flavor and is often served over ice for a chilled, energizing experience.

Mocha

A delicious blend of espresso, steamed milk, and rich chocolate syrup, topped with whipped cream. It combines the boldness of coffee with the sweetness of chocolate, creating a perfect balance of flavors.
